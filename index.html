My Activism Project:
Mental Health Awareness

My website serves as a platform for building a supportive community, emphasizing that individuals are not alone. It features inspiring real-life stories of overcoming trials through TED Talk videos. Additionally, it offers a complimentary wellness assessment, along with a collection of video resources aimed at helping individuals understand and navigate various mental health crises.

The problem that mental health awareness addresses is the pervasive lack of attention, understanding, and support for mental health issues in society. It is a problem because, for far too long, mental health concerns have been stigmatized, ignored, or downplayed, resulting in numerous negative consequences for individuals and society as a whole.



One significant problem related to mental health awareness is the stigma associated with mental illness. Stigma refers to the negative attitudes, stereotypes, and discrimination that people with mental health conditions often face. This issue is problematic for several reasons.



Firstly, stigma creates barriers to seeking help. People with mental health conditions may fear being judged or labeled if they admit to their struggles, causing them to delay or avoid seeking treatment. Consequently, their mental health problems may worsen before they receive support.



Secondly, stigma leads to isolation and shame. Individuals affected by stigma may hide their mental health challenges, which can exacerbate their symptoms and hinder their recovery. This isolation can have profound negative effects on their overall well-being.



These reasons are why I believe my website is a much needed resource that people can benefit from.
